My shared canvas Screenprinting Project

What I Did and What I Learned

In this project, I helped make cool art prints using ink and screens I mixed the colorful ink and pulled a brush tool across the screen to push the paint onto the paper. I learned that you have to press down just right. If you press too hard, the paint squishes everywhere and makes a mess. If you don't press hard enough, the picture looks too light.

Teamwork in new media

I got really good at teamwork during this project. Screenprinting is hard to do myself, so my friends and I had to talk to each other and share what we do At first, we kept messing up and wasting ink. But then, Iwe all helped eachother agree on who should hold the paper and who should do the painting. This helped us work faster, and we learned how to be nice and helpful partner!

Genetics Experiment

What I Did and What I Learned

In this New media project, I got to explore DNA, which is the I used a tiny squirt tool called a pipette to put liquid samples into a gooey gel. Then, we used electricity to move the DNA pieces through the gel. I learned that smaller pieces of DNA can swim through the goo much faster than the big pieces, which lets us see different patterns.

Thinking cruically

I got really good at problem-solving and thinking like a scientist. On our first try, our experiment didn't work and the gel just looked like a big blurry blob. Instead of crying or giving up, I looked at our instructions closely. I figured out that we used the wrong amount of liquid water! On our second try, we fixed it and it worked perfectly. This taught me that making mistakes is just part of science.
