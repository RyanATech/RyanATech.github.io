🎨 "Our Shared Canvas" Screenprinting Project

What I Did and What I Learned

In this project, I helped make cool art prints using ink and screens! I mixed the colorful ink and pulled a squeegee tool across the screen to push the paint onto the paper. I learned that you have to press down just right. If you press too hard, the paint squishes everywhere and makes a mess. If you don't press hard enough, the picture looks too light.

How I Grew and Practiced Teamwork

I got really good at teamwork during this project. Screenprinting is hard to do alone, so my friends and I had to talk to each other and share jobs. At first, we kept messing up and wasting ink. But then, I helped everyone agree on who should hold the paper and who should do the painting. This helped us work faster, and we learned how to be nice and helpful partners!
